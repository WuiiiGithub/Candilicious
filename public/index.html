Your Sweet Path to Smarter Study

Candilicious helps students manage study sessions, track progress, and have fun, fostering a new freedom in learning beyond traditional education.

Learn More

Features That Help You Succeed

Study Session Management

Configure study channels, monitor user activity, track time spent studying, and manage inactivity with warnings and disconnections.

Leaderboards (Coming Soon!)

View local leaderboards within your server and compete on a global leaderboard across all users and servers.

Fun Commands

Inject some fun into your study breaks! Play meme songs and sound effects in your voice channels.

Handling Exception Scenarios

Request temporary 5-minute exceptions for poor network conditions, ensuring uninterrupted study time.

Personal Data Management

Empowers users to manage their own study data, with server data management options if configured.

Community & Support

Contribute to the project, report bugs, suggest features, and join a vibrant community dedicated to learning freedom.

Our Vision for Learning Freedom

At Candilicious, we are strong critics of the current education system. We believe it often stifles creativity, discourages new perspectives, and forces rote memorization over genuine understanding. Our world needs new generations of thinkers, and we observe that current systems don't foster the genius seen in previous eras.

We envision a world where students are truly free in their learning journey. We believe online facilities and recent technologies are gradually dismantling traditional educational barriers, and this is a very positive development. Candilicious is our first step towards creating more opportunities for students to learn and grow freely.

Our Baby Steps:

  • Encourage focused study by kicking inactive users from voice channels.
  • Provide temporary exceptions for users with poor network conditions.
  • Offer fun commands to play meme sound effects and songs in VCs, ensuring a balanced study environment.

Having observed all this, we have a vision of creating more facilities for students to learn and grow. This is our baby steps in this journey. We are not sure how far we can go, but we will try our best. For us, it's not about 'you' or 'me' its about the 'we'. By using the word 'we', we mean people who think and feel the same. It is very important to note that, if we really want to do something great for student community then we need to keep it accessible to student community. And let the things remain accessible to them. So, we are planning our contributions related to these in Open Source.

In a short and a concluding line, we envision a world where the student is free in their learning journey.
